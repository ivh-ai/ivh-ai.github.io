Who I am

I'm a Customer Success Manager at Stampli in Austin, Texas, where I help SMB and mid-market finance teams automate their AP operations. Before that, I spent nearly four years at Oracle guiding companies through full-cycle NetSuite ERP implementations — from kickoff through go-live. I studied International Relations at The University of Texas at Austin, with a business minor from McCombs.

What ties my work together is a simple belief: AI is most powerful when it's used to finish things. Each project on this page started as an idea and ended as something you can actually use — designed, built, debugged, and shipped through an iterative partnership with Claude Code.

Away from the day job, I build. The projects below are what happens when curiosity about AI meets a free weekend — and there are more on the way.
